Ingredients
  1. tri-colored noodles
  2. cucumber
  3. cherry tomato
  4. red onion
  5. ranch
  6. italian dressing (zesty)
Directions
  1. Boil noodles
  2. Chop veggies
  3. Drain noodles
  4. Mix noodles, veggies and ranch and italian dressing
  5. Taste and season to liking
  6. Chill for at least 30 minutes then serve
